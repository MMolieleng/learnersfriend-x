What is Learners Friend

With the current education system, with classes of around 40 or more students, it is easy for some students to fall behind, either because they require a custom learning pace or even special attention to nurture their minds. With the ever-changing world of learning, some curricula have become outdated and therefore it has become necessary to offer some courses that increase relevance to workplaces where some technologies keep changing.

Learners Friend is an online academic marketplace where you can find tutors, courses, and workshops. This is where you can find tutors to help keep your grades up or even ahead or even enroll for a course or join a workshop to learn a skill that may increase your relevance to the market

How it works

Become a Student

To become a student, simply subscribe to a course or particular tutoring session based on what you need to learn. An email will be sent to you with all the relevant details

For Tutors

To become a tutor or to create a course or workshop send us an email to info.learnersfriend@gmail.com with your CV attached, a description of your tutoring sessions or courses to apply
